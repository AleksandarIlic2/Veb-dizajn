Cold chicken with ginger sauce and dried hot peppers

Cold chicken with ginger and dried hot pepper sauce is a refreshing dish that combines juicy chicken with a spicy and aromatic Asian sauce. Ginger is used as a key ingredient in the sauce, adding freshness and a distinctive spicy flavor. Dried hot peppers contribute additional aroma to the dish. This dish is often served cold, making it an ideal choice for summer days or as a light meal.
