Prolećne rolnice sa povrćem i pečurkama

Prolećne rolnice sa povrćem i pečurkama su lagan i osvežavajući azijski zalogaj koji obično kombinuje sveže povrće i aromatične pečurke, umotane u rolnicu. Rolnice se pripremaju tako što se u palačinku slažu sastojci (povrće, pečurke), nakon čega se rolnice savijaju i motaju.Ovaj obrok je idealan za tople dane ili kao lagani obrok koji se lako gricka.Takođe, su popularne kao deo azijskog stila obroka.

Spring rolls with vegetables and mushrooms

Spring rolls with vegetables and mushrooms are a light and refreshing Asian snack that typically combines fresh vegetables and aromatic mushrooms wrapped in a spring roll wrapper. The rolls are prepared by placing ingredients (vegetables, mushrooms) on a pancake, after which the rolls are folded and rolled. This dish is ideal for warm days or as a light meal that is easy to snack on. They are also popular as part of an Asian-style meal.
