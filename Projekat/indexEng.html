New team members

Ross Leo

Ross Leo graduated from the Higher School for Professional Chefs in Beijing. During his studies, he stood out as one of the top students in his cohort. He participated in national culinary competitions and won the gold medal several times. Before coming to Serbia, he gained extensive work experience in 4 and 5-star restaurants and hotels in China. Due to his exceptional knowledge, he decided to leave China and delight people in Serbia with his culinary skills and magical touch.

Anastasija Milanovic

Anastasija Milanović graduated from the Faculty of Economics, specializing in Tourism and Hospitality in Belgrade. During her studies, she excelled and won numerous awards in the field of tourism. Her cheerful and positive energy as a young restaurant manager provides guests with a pleasant experience.

Chef Jin Li

"Master Li is a young and talented chef who started his career at the age of just 10 in his grandmother's restaurant, renowned for the best Chinese soups and desserts in the neighborhood. He graduated from the Higher School for Professional Chefs in Shanghai. He has worked in numerous Chinese restaurants across Europe where he received praise from both guests and restaurant staff. His spirit and motto are to showcase the charms of his grandmother's kitchen to everyone, along with the skills he learned from the world's best chefs during his education."
